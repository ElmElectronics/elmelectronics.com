Integrated Circuit Case Styles

All of our products are offered in either a plastic dual inline package (PDIP) for through-hole applications, or in a small outline integrated circuit (SOIC) style for surface mounting. We shorten the designations on our part numbers for these to P and SM.
Above is the ELM322 integrated circuit, shown in both package styles. When ordering, you would select the appropriate one by buying either the ELM322P, or the ELM322SM.




RoHS and Certificate of Compliance

All of our Integrated Circuits are based on lead-free devices from Microchip Technology Inc. For more information on Microchip products, visit the Microchip web site (http://www.microchip.com/).

Microchip Technology certifies that their products are RoHS compliant. For details, download a copy of their IC and Module RoHS Certificate of Compliance.

We are occasionally asked by our customers to provide a Certificate of Compliance (CoC). The Elm Electronics Certificate of Compliance includes a copy of the Microchip Technology CoC, as well as the Brady Technical Data Sheets for the labelling materials.
